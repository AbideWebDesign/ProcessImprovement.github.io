Large Animal

Description

The OSU Large Animal Hospital specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of complex medical issues in horses, sheep, cattle, camelids, goats, and pigs. The team of large animal experts provide the best possible treatment in state-of-the-art facilities. The Large Animal Clinic also provides mobile vet service through our Rural Veterinary Program.
